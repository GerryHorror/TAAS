Who We Are

We have been operating in the industry for over 20 years and have appropriate tax, accounting, and consulting knowledge. Our consultants are from reputable professional organisations such as SAICA, SAIPA and SAIBA. Additionally, we have tax advisors registered with SAIT.

We rely on our core values of honesty, integrity, confidentiality, professionalism, and trust. We have offices all over South Africa and have entered the international realm since January 2015. Thanks to a global partnership, we have created a reputable brand that represents our core values.

Our tax services include VAT, employee taxes, corporate taxes, individual taxes, estate planning, and general tax planning. We process all tax returns with SARS and respond to any SARS inquiries on your behalf as a tax professional. Our accounting services cover everything from preparing financial statements based on International Financial Reporting Standards (IFRS) to IFRS for Small and Medium-sized Enterprises (SMEs).

We also provide the actual accounting of your company in line with accounting standards and company regulations, from processing through the ledger to the trial balance and, finally, the financial statement. Our consulting services cover everything from helping you register a company with CIPC to starting a business plan, raising capital, and continuing to grow your business.

Our Mission

To provide innovative and cost-effective accounting, tax, and advisory services tailored for tech startups and small businesses. Our team of qualified professionals is dedicated to meeting each client's unique needs, ensuring quality and efficiency at every step.

Our Vision

We aim to be the leading accounting, tax, and advisory services provider for startups and small businesses, driving their growth and success. We aspire to foster long-term partnerships with our clients, built on trust and exceptional service while contributing significantly to job creation and economic development in South Africa.
