Accounting

Our comprehensive suite of services includes monthly management accounts, where we meticulously compile and review your financial data regularly. We also specialise in drafting annual financial statements and conducting independent reviews to ensure accuracy and compliance. Our bookkeeping services are designed to keep your financial records organised and up-to-date. Additionally, we offer expert advice on internal controls affecting your financial statements and guide you on any accounting-related issues you may encounter.

Tax Services

For tax-related services, we prepare and submit all necessary tax submissions, including VAT, employee taxes, and withholding tax. Our team is proficient in using SARS e-filing to submit completed tax returns. We also handle provisional tax preparation and submissions and offer tax planning services, including estate duty. Our expertise extends to addressing any tax-related queries you might have and responding on your behalf to any SARS issues.

Advisory Services

In business advisory, we offer specialised advice to start-up businesses about risks and challenges in the market. We assist with acquisitions and mergers, ensuring a smooth transition and integration process. Our services include assistance with company document submissions for CIPC, including changes to directors, addresses, and memorandum of incorporation, or submitting your new business to CIPC. We prepare annual returns to CIPC and provide them with your annual financial statements. Additionally, we assist with SARS registration for VAT, employee tax, and income tax. Our legal expertise extends to drafting and amending wills, assisting with the dissolution of your business, and advising on compliance with the Companies Act, Tax Act, and financial reporting framework. We also offer B-BBEE services, including issuing certifications and assisting with workman's compensation.
